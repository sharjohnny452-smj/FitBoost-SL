About Fit Boost SL

Fit Boost SL was founded in Freetown with a simple mission: to bring healthy, affordable energy products and practical home solutions to every Sierra Leonean.

We started as a small homemade yogurt producer in 2020, using fresh milk from local Makeni and Bo farms. In 2025, we expanded into smart travel appliances with our 12V car water heater.

Partner With Us

Our Mission

We believe every Sierra Leonean deserves access to healthy food and reliable appliances. That is why we keep our prices affordable and our quality high.

Today, we employ over 30 Sierra Leoneans and partner with 15+ local dairy farms. We are proud to be a Sierra Leonean brand.
